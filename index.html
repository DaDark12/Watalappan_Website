The Story of Watalappan

Delve into the history and cultural significance of Watalappan, a cherished dessert traditionally served during festivals and special occasions in Sri Lanka. Its unique flavor comes from a rich blend of coconut milk, jaggery, eggs, and aromatic spices like cardamom and nutmeg.

The Core Ingredients

The magic of Watalappan lies in its simple yet profound combination of ingredients, each adding a layer of flavor and tradition.

Jaggery

Unrefined palm sugar that provides a deep, caramel-like sweetness and the dessert's signature dark color.

Recipe

Follow these simple steps to make the perfect Watalappan at home. This rich and creamy custard dessert combines coconut milk, jaggery, eggs, and aromatic spices to create a dessert that is loved across generations.

Step 1

Mix jaggery with a little water and melt over low heat. Strain to remove impurities.

Step 2

Whisk eggs and add coconut milk, cardamom, nutmeg, and melted jaggery. Combine well.
