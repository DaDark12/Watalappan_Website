The Royal Recipe

Recreate this classic dessert at home. The secret is in the quality of the jaggery and the richness of the coconut milk.

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, lightly whisk the 4 eggs. Do not over-beat; you don't want it to be frothy.
  2. Add the melted and cooled jaggery to the eggs and mix well.
  3. Pour in the thick coconut milk, ground cardamom, nutmeg, cloves, and salt. Stir gently until just combined.
  4. Strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a heatproof bowl or individual ramekins. This step is key for a smooth, silky texture.
  5. Garnish the top with chopped cashews and raisins.
  6. Cover the bowl/ramekins with aluminum foil to prevent water from dripping in.
