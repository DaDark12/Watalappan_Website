What Is Watalappan?

Watalappan is a traditional Sri Lankan dessert made from coconut milk, jaggery, and aromatic spices like cardamom and nutmeg. Its rich caramel flavor and silky texture make it a national favorite, often enjoyed during Eid and weddings.

Traditional Recipe

  1. Whisk 5 eggs until smooth.
  2. Add 1 cup jaggery syrup and 2 cups coconut milk.
  3. Stir in a pinch of nutmeg and cardamom.
  4. Pour into a mold and steam for 30 minutes.
  5. Cool, garnish with cashews, and serve chilled.
