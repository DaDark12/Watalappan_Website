How to Make Watalappan

Ingredients

  • 1 cup thick coconut milk
  • 3/4 cup jaggery (or dark brown sugar)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg powder
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Melt jaggery gently with coconut milk.
  2. Beat the eggs and mix in the jaggery-coconut blend.
  3. Add spices and salt; strain for smoothness.
  4. Pour into a baking dish, cover, and steam for 25â€“30 minutes.
  5. Chill and serve with nuts on top.
