The Story of Watalappan

Delve into the history and cultural significance of Watalappan, a cherished dessert traditionally served during festivals and special occasions in Sri Lanka. Its unique flavor comes from a rich blend of coconut milk, jaggery, eggs, and aromatic spices like cardamom and nutmeg.

The Core Ingredients

The magic of Watalappan lies in its simple yet profound combination of ingredients, each adding a layer of flavor and tradition.
